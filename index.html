Mobile

San Diego Gas & Electric

The SDG&E iPhone app was launched in 2011, and presented a challenge of content: we did not have access to customers' real-time data to present real-time usage, the obvious best use of an app.

We opted instead to produce a home cost calculator using availabe date on average appliance costs and based the UX workflow on diet and fitness apps, allowing the user to calculate current usage as well as chart ways to reduce their use.

I designed the UI, UX and produced all the graphic assets, ensuring SDG&E's first mobile app took advantage of new (for the company at the time) technology while retaining brand principles and consitency with other digital channels.

To date, the SDG&E iPhone app has gone through a major redesign, added functionality inculding usage presentment, and expanded to the Android market as well.
